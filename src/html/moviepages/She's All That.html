Featured Blu-ray Review: Serendipity and She's All That

This week we have a couple of romantic films that were originally released by Miramax that are now making their Blu-ray debuts: Serendipity and She's All That. Both films came out over a decade ago and both were solid hits at the box office. That is to say, both films made much more than they cost to produce, but well below the $100 million milestone.
